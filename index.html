Welcome to Bina Shah And Associates

Since we
started work in 1997

For more than 25 years, Ar. Bina Shah & Associates has been working as an architectural and planning consultancy firm and is involved in turnkey design and planning solutions for disciplinary projects of diverse nature, ranging from , corporate offices and building housing projects.

The expertise of this firm lies in creating designs and having a deep-rooted understanding ranging from materials used to considerations of the client and the context. We can provide the entire spectrum of services coming under buildings & infrastructure from concept to completion with macro and micro-planning of all aspects.

At Bina Shah & Associates, we have expertise in providing the following services:

  • Architecture – Building and Green Building design
  • Interior Design – facilities design for Corporate, Commercial, Residential spaces, Hospitals & Clinics.

About Founder

Principal
Architect

Ar. Bina Shah graduated from the Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda in 1988. The current practice, Bina Shah & Associates is based in Baroda & is led by her as the Principal Architect.

Bina Shah has vast experience in the design & implementation of projects of diverse nature, from Housing, Interior Design, Offices and Adaptive Re-Use.
